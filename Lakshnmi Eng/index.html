About Lakshmi Engineering

Three decades of engineering excellence, innovation, and commitment to quality manufacturing in Bangalore.

Lakshmi Engineering has been at the forefront of precision metal fabrication in Bangalore since 1995. With over 30 years of industry experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses across automotive, aerospace, defense, infrastructure, and industrial manufacturing sectors.

Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with advanced CNC machinery, laser cutting systems, and automated welding technologies. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern engineering practices to deliver products that meet the highest international quality standards including ISO 9001:2015 certification.

From concept to completion, we provide comprehensive solutions including design assistance, prototyping, production, and post-production services. Our team of skilled engineers and technicians work collaboratively with clients to understand their unique requirements and deliver customized solutions that exceed expectations.
